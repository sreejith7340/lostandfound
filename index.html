How It Works

Simple 3-Step Process

Get started in minutes. Our streamlined process makes it easy to report and recover items.

1
Report an Item

Lost something? Found something? Fill out a quick form with the item details, location, and your contact info.

2
Admin Reviews

Our admin team reviews and approves the report, making it visible to the campus community.

3
Get Reunited

Submit a claim request. Once verified, the item gets returned to its rightful owner.

Features

Everything You Need

A complete system built to handle every aspect of lost and found management on campus.

Smart Search

Search and filter items by name, category, date, or location to find what you're looking for quickly.

Image Upload

Admin Verified

Every report is reviewed and approved by campus administrators before going live on the platform.

Submit a claim with proof of ownership. Our admin team reviews and facilitates the safe return of items.
